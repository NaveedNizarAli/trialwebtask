We’re currently looking for drivers who meet our Spruce Box Now Team Requirements

A Spruce Box Now Team Member is:

  • 18-Years or older, and holds a valid state-issued ID
  • Authorized to work in The US
  • Able to lift and carry up to 25, 50, 75 lbs depending on vehicle size
  • Has a iPhone 6 or Android capable of running our Spruce Box App
  • Has a checking account to receive direct deposits
  • Has a Spruce Box Now Approved Vehicle

In addition, a Spruce Box Now Driver has:

  • A car, truck, or cargo van
  • An active driver’s license, vehicle registration, and applicable automobile insurance valid in the state they are working and/or residing in
  • A clean driving and criminal record

All Spruce Box Now Vehicles must:

  • Be less than 15 years old
  • Pass a Spruce Box Vehicle Inspection
  • No salvage titles are allowed

Please Note: All Spruce Box Team Members must pass a mandatory background check.
